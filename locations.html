Detailed Travel Spots

Kolapathana Ella Waterfall üíß

#Waterfalls #Hiking #MustSee

Kolapathana Ella is the region's largest and most famous waterfall, cascading down multiple tiers. The hike to reach the base is moderately challenging but highly rewarding, taking you through picturesque tea estates and dense forest patches. It's an ideal spot for photography and enjoying the cool, misty environment.

Balagolla Mountain Range Viewpoint üåÑ

#Viewpoint #Sunrise #Trekking

Offering stunning panoramic views of the village and the surrounding valleys, the Balagolla viewpoint is perfect for catching a sunrise or watching the famous mist roll in. This area borders the Pidurutalagala forest reserve and is a haven for trekkers seeking high-altitude tranquility.

#Bathing #Nature #Relaxation

The clear, ice-cold waters of the Belihul Oya stream, which originates from the mountain range, offer several natural rock pools perfect for a refreshing dip on a warm day. It's a peaceful spot where you can blend into the sounds of nature.
